Liz Ogumbo Wines™

Driven by a vision of premiumisation, Liz Ogumbo Wines leads the way in merging wine with lifestyle — fusing education, hospitality, entertainment, tourism, fashion, music & art.

About

Each bottle is paired with an original soundtrack, turning every sip into a wine-musical experience. Scan the QR code on every label and step into a world where wine meets music in perfect harmony.

At Liz Ogumbo Wines, we take the time to understand our customers' needs and offer an extensive selection of quality vintage wines, developed and distributed so customers can enjoy the highest quality products at the most competitive prices. Since incorporation in 2018, we've built a proven track record for sales in South Africa and Kenya, with licensing to export to other global markets.

Whether it's a romantic dinner, a family gathering, or a business event, we focus on creating a unique and unforgettable customer experience. Don't just drink wine — experience wine.
