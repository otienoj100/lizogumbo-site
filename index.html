About Liz

One woman, five ecosystems.

For over two decades Liz has turned ideas into brands, platforms and products that connect African creativity with global audiences — spanning fashion, music, wine, storytelling and circular innovation. The thread running through all of it: culture, made into intellectual property.

Founder of

2005
Founded Imani Models, professionalizing Kenya's modelling industry.

2010
Released the debut KenSoul™ album, founding the philosophy.

Founded Fashion Lab Africa™, a pioneering fashion media platform.

Music & Philosophy

KenSoul™

More than a genre — KenSoul™ is a musical identity and creative philosophy blending African heritage with global sound through storytelling, rhythm and performance.

Rooted in jazz, taarab, chakacha, blues, reggae, hip hop and world music, it's the foundation of Liz's catalogue and artistic voice.

Liz Ogumbo Fashion™

African heritage. Circular creativity. Conscious luxury.

The original creative platform where Liz explores identity and heritage through design — merging craftsmanship and narrative for the globally conscious consumer.

Nearly two decades of ready-to-wear, couture and advocacy-driven collections laid the foundation for DeniMania™ and the patented 3R Methodology™.

Circular Couture

DeniMania™

Where circular fashion meets cultural innovation. DeniMania™ transforms discarded denim into fashion, music, storytelling, education and economic opportunity.

Powered by the patented 3R Methodology™, it evolves through collections, training programmes and immersive experiences that bridge creativity and community.

"We don't see waste. We see stories waiting to be retold."
— Liz Ogumbo
