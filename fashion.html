Liz Ogumbo Fashion™

African heritage. Circular creativity. Conscious luxury. The original creative platform where Liz explores identity and heritage through design.

Liz Ogumbo Fashion™ is where a design career built on craftsmanship and narrative began. Since 2007, the label has merged Kenyan heritage with contemporary silhouette, producing ready-to-wear and couture pieces worn by a globally conscious audience.

Bold, elegant and timeless — the collections embrace self-definition through sleek cuts and provocative simplicity, with an attitude that comes from the inside.

Nearly two decades of collections, runway presentations and advocacy-driven design work laid the creative and technical foundation for DeniMania™ and the patented 3R Methodology™ — proof that a fashion house can be both a design studio and a research lab.

What defines the label

  • Ready-to-wear and couture rooted in Kenyan craftsmanship
  • Design language that treats heritage as a living, evolving material
  • A runway and editorial practice that doubles as cultural commentary
  • The design R&D behind DeniMania™'s circular couture
