KenSoul™

Not a genre. Not simply an album. A musical identity, a philosophy, a creative framework — the foundation of Liz Ogumbo's musical catalogue.

Liz Ogumbo is an acclaimed African world-jazz-fusion artist known for KenSoul™ — a rich tapestry of influences drawn from jazz, taarab, chakacha, blues, reggae and hip hop. Her sound moves through Luo, Swahili, English, French, Spanish and Portuguese, weaving her Kenyan roots into a genre-agnostic musical language.

At the core of her songwriting is her Kenyan Soul — her KenSoul — a descriptor that captures who Liz is today: her roots and culture, her contribution to music and fashion, and her passion for Africa.
